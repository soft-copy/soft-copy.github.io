Process

Copywriting Project Procedures

This describes how we can expect to work together to achieve your goals.

Initial Contact

Please contact me to let me know you are interested in discussing a project (or projects).

George P. “Jud” Taylor IX

+1-575-347-1599

taylor@softwarecopywriting.com

Discovery Questionnaire

Once I learn of interest in work, I will send an emnail with a Discovery Questionnaire, to learn what we will need to share to make sure a project succeeds.

The answers will help define the scope of your project, and to help me know more about you and your company. This will set us on the right track to achieve your goals.

Please answer the questions and send them back as soon as possible.

Most clients return the form within one to three business days.

Launch Consultation

I will review your answers and build a short Project Plan, to help clarify requirements to all involved.

We will schedule a 15 to 20 minute Launch Consultation 3 to 10 days after I get your answers. During the Launch Consultation we will discuss the answers and the project plan. I will particularly listen to any more direction and insight you have.

Investment and Start of Work

After the call, I will draft and send a Services Agreement.

If you have a preferred Agreement, we can use that. The agreement will contain specifics of your project, outline what I will deliver (with deadlines), and list your investment for the project and terms of payment. (Please note: I require 50% of the project total prior to start. Most clients prefer to pay via bank transfer.)

I will start work on your project as soon as we have both signed the Agreement and 50% payment has cleared.

Communication During Project

As we work together, quick and clear communication will be important. The best way to contact me is email, at taylor@softwarecopywriting.com. I respond to these emails within 24 hours.

Although email is usually best, I can be reached +1-575-347-1599, weekdays from 8:30 AM until 5:30 PM Eastern Time, unless I am in a meeting or cannot talk on a phone.

If I do not answer, please leave a voicemail with your name and phone number. I will return your call as soon as I can.

For longer scheduled calls, we can arrange a voice or video call, through Skype or some other tool.

Project Research

Research will help me understand your product, your voice, and, as relevant, your customer’s core emotional purchase drivers. I will also work to discover your customer’s buying process and identify their core needs and perspectives. This will be to position you as the best solution to those needs.

Research will begin with a review of your website, material provided by you, and other relevant materials.

If required (as for case studies and white papers or special reports), I will interview key members of your team, and perhaps one or more of your customers.

As needed, I will reach out to you for any additional details, and other resources.

I will treat your information with appropriate care.

Collaboration

Communication will be key. I appreciate that you will likely be very busy. Some clients like to have me work on their project and wait until I have completed the first draft, with very little communication until then. Others prefer a more hands-on approach, dealing with me on a daily or bi-weekly basis. I am happy either way.

Which would you prefer?

Let me know

  • how often you would like to communicate, and
  • the best way for me to reach you.

To minimize confusion, having a single point of contact (besides you) in your organization will be very important. I will normally communicate directly with that person. They should then coordinate with other team members to gather information and approvals.

Let me know that single point of contact during the project(s).

Review of First Draft

I use software with edit-tracking to share drafts. This will let us track changes, make comments, and collaborate. I do not recommend using Google Docs. They have, in some cases, locked users out of their own documents. Also, Google scans documents stored on its servers for its own purposes.

As part of the Agreement, we will decide on deadlines for first drafts. Once I send first drafts, please review each carefully. I strongly urge you to also ask team members to review the document. When reviewing drafts, checking tone, message, and any offer is critical.

Based on feedback, I will be able to fine-tune work for second (and, if required) third drafts.

Revisions

Revisions are expected. We will work together to get a solid understanding of your needs, which will help me write effective copy. If you are not 100% satisfied with the project, I will revise it to your specifications.

One or two revisions will be made on a complimentary basis, provided these revisions are requested within thirty days of receipt of the project. Additional revisions, or revisions requested after thirty days, might incur additional charges.

My goal is not to minimize or maximize revisions, but rather to contribute work that will earn a glowing testimonial.

Final Approval

Once I have made all revisions, I will send a final draft to you. I ask that you then affirm by email that work is complete. Once I receive this from you, I will send you an invoice for the balance due.

The invoice will be due upon receipt.

As a professional, I will respond promptly to any requests, and I ask you to do the same with my invoice.

Review of Final Production

You may want to send the final copy to a designer for formatting. I will be happy to check final versions after any graphical elements have been added.

I am also happy to work with your design team to make changes needed to make sure the final piece will be as effective as possible.

Following Projects

Once a project is complete, I offer a complimentary “hot wash-up” consultation. In these we spend a few minutes talking about what went well, and where improvements could be made. We also can review the Discovery Questionnaire and talk about other ways to work toward yout goals.

Where appropriate, I am happy to map out new project plans to ensure you continue to have high-quality, consistent copy to achieve your goals and grow your business.
